Bio

He has worked in many different domains with numerous programming paradigms, languages, technologies and platforms throughout his career. Most recently, he has been working in eye-tracking, sensor informatics and high frequency message-oriented middleware. Bens first foray into programming was with BASIC on the Commodore64 and BBC Micro A/B.
Ben current position is as Senior Software Engineer at the CSIRO, Autonomous Systems Laboratory but this blog, its content and opinions are entirely his own. They do not reflect the opinions of his employer.
