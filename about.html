About me

Ben Howell is a software engineer from Hobart, Tasmania and when not hacking, likes to spend time mountain biking or tinkering in his workshop whilst not achieving very much. He has worked in many different domains with numerous programming languages, technologies and paradigms throughout his career.

Most recently, he has been working in eye-tracking, sensor informatics and high frequency message-oriented middleware. Ben's first foray into programming was with BASIC on the Commodore64 and BBC Micro A/B.

Ben's current position is as Senior Software Engineer at the CSIRO, Autonomous Systems Laboratory but this blog, its content and opinions are entirely his own. They do not reflect the opinions of his employer.
